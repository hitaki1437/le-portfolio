BRANDING

Here, you'll see my first ever branding that I've created for my personal projects. I kind of want to try creating a brand and follow the standard process of development and it was pretty fun experience because I'm learning the tools at the same time. I can't wait to create more branding for my personal projects.

UNIE

UNIE is a video game developer, and publisher. It was founded in June 2024 by Renny Melh Roldan.
(Take note that this is only my personal project.)

Logo Design

Starting off with logo. I decided to choose my first brand with 3 different color palette and utilize typography and minimalist on this logo to maintain the simplicity.

Mockups Design

Moving on Mockups design. I included business card, coffee-cup, box, t-shirt, and tote bag. Adding your design to mockup template was satifying once you implement the target design completely.

Imagery

This process was really simple to follow. It just depends on how are you going to create any type of design/layout to match the company's identity. Adding this simple text with color grade was satifying to feel because you could notice the process of your branding was slowly getting a progress.

Typeface

Including typeface was really important value to represent your branding and it also act as tone of your brand and I decided to choose Satoshi from Sans serif, because it is commonly used by tech companies including facebook and google.
